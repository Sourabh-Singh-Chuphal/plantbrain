NEAR-MISS INCIDENT REPORT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Report No.      : NMR-2022-031
Date of Incident: 07 August 2022
Time of Incident: 10:15 IST
Location        : Bay 2 — Overhead Crane Bay
Equipment Tag   : CR-03 (Demag 20T Overhead Crane)
Report Prepared : Priya Nair, HSE Manager
Date Submitted  : 08 August 2022

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. INCIDENT DESCRIPTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
On 07 August 2022 at 10:15 IST, the overhead crane CR-03 (20 tonne, Bay 2) experienced
an uncontrolled swing of a suspended load (steel coil, estimated 4.2 tonnes) during a
routine lift. The load swung approximately 1.8 metres from its intended path and came
within 0.4 metres of Operator Suresh Kumar who was walking below the load path.

Crane operator Ganesh Bankar stopped the lift immediately. Suresh Kumar was not struck.
No injury occurred. This event is classified as NEAR-MISS — Category 2 (High Potential).

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2. IMMEDIATE CAUSES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
(a) The lift was commenced before the load path area was confirmed clear of pedestrians.
    Per Lift Procedure LP-02, the signalman (Vijay Patil) was required to confirm area
    clear before Ganesh Bankar commenced the lift. Vijay Patil had not completed the
    area clearance check.

(b) Suresh Kumar entered the lift path zone without verifying crane status.
    Bay 2 crane activity was in progress (amber beacon active). Suresh Kumar stated
    he was taking a shortcut and did not look for the amber beacon.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3. ROOT CAUSES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
(a) Physical barriers below load path are not required by current procedure but would
    have prevented pedestrian access.
(b) Suresh Kumar had not attended crane safety refresher training (last attended: 2019).

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4. CORRECTIVE ACTIONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CR-01 | Install temporary barrier tape below CR-03 lift paths during all lifts. Immediate.
CR-02 | Suresh Kumar to attend Crane Safety Awareness refresher. Due: 31 Aug 2022.
CR-03 | Signalman pre-lift checklist to include area clearance signature. Due: 22 Aug 2022.
CR-04 | Incident shared at next safety meeting. Due: 14 August 2022.
