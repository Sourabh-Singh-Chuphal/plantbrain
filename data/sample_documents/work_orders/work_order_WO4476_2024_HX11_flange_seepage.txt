───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. WORK DESCRIPTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Investigation of odour noted at HX-11 south shell flange during Night Shift of 19 August
2024, per Shift Handover Log SHL-2024-0819. Odour described as "faint gas smell near
south flange, not confirmed as leak."

This work order is linked to WO-4471 (GB-14 investigation) — the elevated GB-14 reading
on 19 August 2024 was attributed in part to a possible HX-11 flange seepage. WO-4476
is to confirm or rule out the HX-11 source.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2. WORK PERFORMED
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Date: 23 August 2024

Step 1 — Soap bubble test on HX-11 south flange joints (Flange #5, #6, #7, #8):
          Flange #7: Small bubble formation observed at 3 o'clock position. Minor seepage.
          Flanges #5, #6, #8: No bubbles. No leak.

Step 2 — Portable gas reading at HX-11 Flange #7: 4% LEL methane. Source confirmed.

Step 3 — Flange #7 bolts: 4 of 8 bolts at reduced torque (measured: 65–72 Nm vs. 110 Nm
          specification). Two bolts showing corrosion on threads.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3. REMEDIAL ACTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Bolts retorqued to 110 Nm. Post-retorque soap test: No bubbles. Seepage appears resolved.
Portable gas at Flange #7: 0.5% LEL. Acceptable.

⚠ IMPORTANT NOTE: Flange #7 gasket (spiral-wound, 150# ANSI class) should be replaced
at next planned shutdown. The gasket shows surface fretting from repeated thermal cycling
and the bolt torque loss indicates potential creep. Retorquing is a TEMPORARY FIX only.

Recommended: Schedule gasket replacement at next Bay 3 planned maintenance window
(Q4 2024 or Q1 2025 shutdown).

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4. CLOSURE STATUS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CLOSED — PARTIAL. Immediate seepage resolved by retorquing. Gasket replacement required
as permanent fix. Planned maintenance team notified verbally by Rajiv Sharma.
Work Order for gasket replacement: NOT RAISED (to be raised at next planned shutdown).

[Post-incident note added 22 January 2026: Gasket replacement was NOT completed.
 HX-11 Flange #7 gasket failure occurred on 21 January 2026 and was identified as
 the methane source in WO-4892. The failure to raise a formal follow-up work order
 for gasket replacement is identified as a contributing factor in RCA-2026-007.]
