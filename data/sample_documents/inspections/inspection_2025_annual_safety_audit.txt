EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2025 represents Vindhya Steelworks' safest year in the past 5 years by number of
recordable incidents. Zero Lost Time Injuries (LTIs). Two Near-Miss events recorded
(NMR-2025-003, NMR-2025-018 — both unrelated to gas detection).

Despite overall improvement, this audit identified three legacy corrective actions
from prior incidents that remain OPEN and OVERDUE:

(A) CA-04 / NMR-2019-047: Digital calibration tracking and permit cross-check system.
    Raised: March 2019. Status: OPEN (6+ years). Multiple follow-up deadlines missed.
    Risk: Manual calibration records create risk of sensor degradation going undetected.

(B) SIR-2021-034-03: Follow up on CA-04 from NMR-2019-047. Status: OPEN.

(C) WO-4476 (Aug 2024): HX-11 Flange #7 gasket replacement.
    Verbal instruction given to Maintenance team. No formal Work Order raised.
    Status: OPEN — gasket not yet replaced as of this audit date.
    Risk: Recurring seepage possible if thermal cycling continues.
